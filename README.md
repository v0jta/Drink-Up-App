# Drink-Up-App
Data summarizing a list of places in Prague where, according to a generally binding ordinance, it is forbidden to drink alcoholic beverages in public

[GENERALLY BINDING ORDINANCE of the City of Prague,
on the prohibition of the consumption of alcoholic beverages in public spaces](https://praha.eu/documents/d/praha/obecne_zavazna_vyhlaska_c_10_2022_o_zakazu_alko_napoju_3453383)

the data has been mined using
- [Overpass turbo](https://overpass-turbo.eu)
- [OpenStreetMaps](https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=19/50.08637/14.41511)
- [geopandas](https://overpass-turbo.eu)https://geopandas.org/en/stable/ and OSMnx Python packages

and can be viewed via [**GeoJSON.io**](https://geojson.io) by copiing [the "PRG1_merged.geojson" file](PRG1_merged.geojson) into the website




# Vyhláška 

Požívání alkoholických nápojů se zakazuje
- na veřejných prostranstvích nebo jejich částech podle vyhlášky
- na dětských hřištích a pískovištích a v okruhu 100 metrů od nich
- v okruhu 100 metrů od škol a školských zařízení
- v okruhu 100 metrů od zdravotnických zařízení
- v okruhu 100 metrů od stanic metra, a to od - uzavíratelných vstupů do vestibulů stanic, které jsou určeny pouze ke vstupu a výstupu cestujících;
- uzavíratelných vstupů do stanic, jejichž vestibuly slouží též jako podchody pro pěší
- v prostoru nástupních ostrůvků nebo samostatných nástupišť veřejné linkové dopravy, včetně přístřešků pro cestující umístěných mimo nástupní ostrůvky nebo samostatná nástupiště veřejné linkové dopravy; V případě umístění nástupiště u hrany chodníku zákaz platí ještě 5 metrů za označníkem a 5 metrů za koncem nástupiště, a to vždy v celé šíři chodníku. Není-li konec označen, zákaz platí 65 metrů od označníku do prostoru nástupiště.

- zahrádky restaurací

- prostor, ve kterém se koná veřejnosti přístupná slavnost zejména vinobraní, posvícení či jiná tradiční, kulturní, taneční, sportovní a podobná akce

# Englis translation of The Decree
The consumption of alcoholic beverages is prohibited
- in public spaces or their parts according to the decree
- on children's playgrounds and sandpits and within a radius of 100 meters from them
- within a radius of 100 meters from schools and educational institutions
- within a radius of 100 meters from medical facilities
- within a radius of 100 meters from metro stations, specifically from:
   - closable entrances to station vestibules, which are intended solely for passenger entry and exit;
   - closable entrances to stations, whose vestibules also serve as pedestrian underpasses
- in the area of boarding islands or separate platforms of public line transport, including passenger shelters located outside boarding islands or separate platforms of public line transport; In the case of a platform located at the edge of the sidewalk, the prohibition applies 5 meters beyond the marker and 5 meters beyond the end of the platform, always covering the full width of the sidewalk. If the end is not marked, the prohibition applies 65 meters from the marker into the platform area.

Exceptions:
- restaurant terraces
- spaces directly adjacent to refreshment stands and at markets where the sale of alcoholic beverages is permitted by market regulations
- areas where public festivities take place, particularly grape harvest festivals, fairs, or other traditional, cultural, dance, sports, and similar events
- New Year's Eve and New Year's Day celebrations on January 1st and December 31st


f) An alcoholic beverage is a drink containing more than 0.5% ethanol by volume.
(1) A medical facility refers to spaces designated for providing health services.
(3) Types of schools include kindergarten, elementary school, secondary school (grammar school, secondary vocational school, and secondary vocational training institute), conservatory, higher vocational school, elementary art school, and language school with the right to conduct state language exams. The Ministry will determine by implementing regulation the types of schools according to their specialization for the purposes of their designation.
(4) An educational institution provides services and education that complement or support education in schools or are directly related to it, or it ensures institutional and protective upbringing or preventive educational care1a) (hereinafter referred to as "educational services"). The educational institution implements education according to the school educational program mentioned in § 5 para. 2.
(5) Types of educational institutions include facilities for further education of pedagogical staff, educational advisory institutions, educational institutions for extracurricular education, special-purpose educational institutions, educational and boarding institutions, school catering facilities, educational institutions for institutional or protective upbringing, and educational institutions for preventive educational care. The division of educational institutions for institutional or protective upbringing and educational institutions for preventive educational care is determined by a special legal regulation1a).
